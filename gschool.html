Where do I go from here?

For the past 7+ years I have been working at a corporate level within the Dog Industry. While I have greatly enjoyed my time there, I decided a couple years ago that I wanted a change and that brought me here to gSchool.
Though I have almost no experience in coding and/or software development, I did take a class in web design years ago and some things from it have stuck with me. I love the feeling you get when you write code and then see that code come to life on the web. I love the never-ending possibilities for what you can do and how many people you can reach with it.
gSchool is the perfect place for me to start the next story of my life. I completely agree with their approach used with regards to how long it takes to become a software developer and best practices for fully understanding the material.
It is my most sincere hope that I will be able to graduate from the gSchool and create something (or work for someone) that is doing a service for the community. Here we go!
